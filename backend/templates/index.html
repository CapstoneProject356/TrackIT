WHO WE ARE

Smart. Secure. Reliable Attendance.

TrackIT is an intelligent attendance management system built to eliminate proxy attendance and manual inefficiencies. By combining GPS verification, face recognition, and dynamic QR codes, TrackIT ensures accurate, transparent, and real-time attendance tracking for modern educational institutions.
